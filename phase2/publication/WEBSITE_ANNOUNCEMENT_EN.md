# OCLM Phase 2 Official Release

WonderMetanism Inc. announces the official release of Phase 2 of the Oracle–Child Lineage Model (OCLM), conceived and designed by Hirofumi Kureha (HiRO).

## Final result

Under the stated symbolic models and assumptions:

- 80/80 tested symbolic adversarial attack classes were ruled out;
- zero counterexample traces were found;
- zero attack classes remained unresolved;
- 8/8 health and reachability properties were reachable; and
- A48 H1/H2/H3 and the final property were verified together in one integrated Tamarin invocation.

OCLM distinguishes record acceptance from canonical lineage. A different record, identifier, certificate, ledger history, or consensus outcome does not become the canonical lineage of an existing Child merely by being recorded or accepted as such.

OCLM does not replace blockchain consensus. It is intended to operate as a higher lineage layer determining whether states accepted by different systems belong to the same authenticated origin and succession history.
