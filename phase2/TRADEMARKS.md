## 3. Permitted Factual Reference

Without separate authorization, you may use the names
Oracle–Child Lineage Model and OCLM solely as reasonably
necessary to:

- identify the original OCLM publication;
- cite OCLM in research or educational materials;
- discuss, review, criticize, or compare OCLM;
- report independent verification results;
- link to the canonical OCLM source;
- accurately describe non-commercial research concerning OCLM.

Such references must be truthful and must not imply:

- sponsorship by WonderMetanism Inc.;
- approval by WonderMetanism Inc.;
- certification or official compliance;
- partnership or organizational affiliation;
- authorization for commercial use.

Where reasonably practical, factual references should include:

Oracle–Child Lineage Model (OCLM)<br>
Conceived and designed by Hirofumi Kureha (HiRO)<br>
First published by WonderMetanism Inc.
