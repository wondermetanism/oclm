Verified guarantee:

A revoked certificate cannot be reactivated without prior revocation-
authority compromise. A verifier holding the current revocation state r1
does not accept a revoked certificate. Acceptance of a revoked certificate
requires either a missing revocation update or prior verifier compromise.

intervening verifier compromise.

- certificate state active -> revoked
- finite revocation verifier states r0 -> r1

- revocation-authority compromise
